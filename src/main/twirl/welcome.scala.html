Scale Camp 2012

Scale Camp is an unconference aimed at discussing the most difficult problems in scalability.

Gathering together some of the brightest names in web engineering, it allows a free and frank discussion about almost any form of scaling problem, from operational aspects of running a datacenter; picking an appropriate NoSql solution; to scaling your engineering team
